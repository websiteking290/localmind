Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an internet connection?

No! LocalMind works entirely offline. You only need internet for the initial setup (or to download additional models later). Once set up, everything runs locally on your computer.

What are the minimum system requirements?

Windows 10+ or macOS 12+, 8GB RAM (16GB recommended), a USB 3.0 port, and any modern CPU. No GPU required — everything runs on your processor.

Can I add more models later?

Yes! The USB has ~100GB of free space. You can download additional models from our catalog using the built-in update system. Some are free, some are paid.

How fast are the responses?

On a modern CPU (Intel i5/i7, AMD Ryzen, Apple M1+), expect 5-20 tokens per second depending on the model. Smaller models like Gemma 3 are faster; larger models like Phi-4 are slower but more capable.

Is my data private?

Completely. Your conversations never leave your device. There's no cloud service, no data mining, no tracking. Everything stays on the USB drive.
